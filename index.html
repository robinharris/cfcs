Run by the community for the community

  • run entirely by volunteers
  • wide range of products and services
  • serving the village, surrounding areas and visitors
  • investing in the shop and village
The shop received the Queen's Award for Volunteering in 2022 - the highest level of recognition for voluntary organistions. Jo Ropner, the Lord Lieutenant of Yorkshire, presented the glass crystal, now proudly displayed in the shop, at a ceremony accompanied by a special cake. Around 60 volunteers toasted the honour and then enjoyed a takeaway supper.
