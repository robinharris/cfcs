We are proud to operate entirely with volunteers - no-one takes any payment for their work. The shop is highly professional and always aims to achieve best-practice. We pay keen attention to minimising our impact on the environment; promoting recycling and minimising single use packaging.

Our volunteers are the shop

There are opportunties to volunteer in several roles - your choice and no commitment. There is a weekly rota to sign up for any shifts you fancy (mostly 2 hours).

Who can volunteer?

We welcome everyone. Volunteers between 14 and 18 are not allowed to sell certain items so need to work with someone, else or volunteer for other roles

Activities you can volunteer for

  • Serving
  • Opening / Closing
  • Newspaper sorting
  • Receiving stock from suppliers
  • Re-stocking shelves
  • Weighing out bulk items
  • Labelling
  • Gardening
  • Cleaning
  • Maintenance

Our team of volunteers is is a self-help group and frequently brings together people who would not otherwise meet each other. Social events are organised regularly including Christmas parties, summer fayres and anytime there is something to celebrate. The work is fun, satisfying and enjoying time to relax with friends is so rewarding

Most training is done on the job, working with an experienced volunteer.

The till uses a simple touch screen and most items are scanned for pricing.

Specific training is provided as appropriate

A few topics need to be covered to meet regulations e.g. selling cigarettes and alcohol. A short training session will be provided on one of your shifts
