We're thrilled to have an entirely volunteer-based team at the shop. Everyone here is dedicated to creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where people come first. Plus, we're committed to minimizing our environmental impact, so you'll find that we're big supporters of recycling and reducing single use packaging. Come check us out and see for yourself!

If you're looking to get involved and give back to your community, we've got plenty of opportunities for you to volunteer with us. You can choose the role that works best for you and there's no commitment required. Plus, we have a weekly rota where you can sign up for shifts that fit your schedule (most of them are just two hours). We'd love to have you join us and make a difference!

We welcome everyone to volunteer with us, regardless of age. If you're between 14 and 18, there are some items that you won't be able to sell, but don't worry! You can still volunteer with us in other roles or work alongside someone else. We're just happy to have you on board and contribute your time and talents to our team.

  • Serving
  • Opening / Closing
  • Newspaper sorting
  • Receiving stock from suppliers
  • Re-stocking shelves
  • Weighing out bulk items
  • Labelling
  • Gardening
  • Cleaning
  • Maintenance

We're a friendly group of volunteers who enjoy coming together to make a difference and have a good time. Our team is a great way to meet new people and make connections with others you might not have otherwise had the chance to meet. Plus, the work we do is enjoyable and satisfying. To top it all off, we have regular social events like Christmas parties and summer fayres where we can celebrate and relax with our friends. It's a rewarding and enjoyable experience all around, and we'd love for you to join us!

If you're interested in volunteering with us, you'll be happy to know that most of our training is done on the job. This means that you'll have the opportunity to work closely with an experienced volunteer who can guide you through the process and help you get up to speed. It's a great way to learn and gain hands-on experience in a supportive and welcoming environment. We hope you'll consider joining us!.
